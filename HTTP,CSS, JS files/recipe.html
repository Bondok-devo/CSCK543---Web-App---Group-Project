Spaghetti Bolognese

Category: Main Course

Cooking Time: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g minced beef
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in boiling water.
  2. Fry minced beef until brown.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
